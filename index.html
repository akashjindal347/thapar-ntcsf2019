Number Theory, Combinatorics, & Special Functions

Conference in honour of 70th birthday of Prof. A. K. Agarwal

Symposia on Applied Mathematics and Engg. Applications

About The Conference

School of Mathematics and Computer Applications, Thapar University, is organizing the XII Annual conference of Society for Special Functions and their Applications. This Conference will provide a common platform for interaction, exchange of ideas and latest development in the field of Special Functions and various related fields of Mathematical Sciences. The day to day activities during the conference are designed to be interactive, involving sessions like plenary lectures, invited talks and paper presentation sessions, covering a wide range of topics including Fractional Calculus, Number theory, Combinatorics, Special Functions, Lie Theory, Orthogonal Polynomials, q-Theory etc.
